The Problem

Administrative Chaos is
Hurting Patient Outcomes

Therapy centers and care providers lose 20-30% of productivity to fragmented, manual processes. Specialists spend time on bureaucracy instead of their patients.

📄

Data Silos

Patient records scattered across physical notebooks, Excel files, WhatsApp groups, and paper calendars. No single source of truth.

📈

No Data-Driven Decisions

Without digital instruments, patient progress is nearly impossible to quantify objectively, leading to sub-optimal interventions.

⚠️

Errors & Opacity

Manual billing, scheduling conflicts, and zero transparency for parents. Human errors compound across every workflow.

The Solution

One Platform. Complete Control.

TempoApp acts as a "Single Source of Truth" synchronizing three critical pillars in real-time.

💼

Resource Management (ERP)

Staff scheduling, service configuration, role-based access, and automated billing in one integrated system.

📊

Progress Monitoring (Analytics & EHR)

Track patient evolution with structured programs, intervention plans, goal achievement metrics, and visual analytics.

👪

Family Engagement (CRM)

Dedicated Parent Portal with session schedules, progress tracking, shared documents, invoices, and real-time messaging.

Product Overview

Two Portals, One Ecosystem

Purpose-built experiences for staff and families, connected through a shared real-time database.

Staff Portal

Admin · Coordinator · Therapist
  • KPI dashboard with today's sessions & pending tasks
  • Multi-view calendar (month, week, day) with filters
  • Client profiles with programs & intervention plans
  • Team management with workload analytics
  • Invoice generation & payment tracking
  • Real-time internal messaging
  • Analytics: revenue, attendance, utilization
  • Push notifications for schedule changes

Parent Portal

Anonymous secure access · No account needed
  • Code-based login (GDPR-friendly, no personal data)
  • Upcoming & past session schedule
  • Child's progress with success rate metrics
  • Shared documents (reports, evaluations)
  • Invoice history with PDF download
  • Direct messaging with the therapy team

Competitive Edge

What Makes TempoApp Different

Most competitors are adapted accounting software. We built around the Intervention Plan.

  • 01

    Clinical-First Architecture

    Logic is built around therapy programs and patient goals, not just calendars. Progress tracking is native, not bolted on.

  • 02

    Privacy by Design

    Parents access via anonymous tokens — no account creation, minimal data collection. Maximum GDPR compliance by default.

  • 03

    PWA / Offline-First

    Works on any device including old tablets. Therapists can log data during home visits without internet — syncs automatically on reconnection.

  • 04

    AI-Ready Analytics

    Architecture designed for ML integration: predictive analytics on patient progress and smart scheduling optimization.

  • 05

    Seamless Billing Integration

    Native SmartBill and e-Factura (Romania) integration. A completed session auto-generates invoices with zero manual entry.

  • 06

    Modular & Multi-Sector

    Same core platform adapts to therapy, education, social care, and corporate wellness through configurable modules.
