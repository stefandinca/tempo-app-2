The work after the work

Most centers run on a patchwork of notebooks, spreadsheets, and WhatsApp groups. The therapy is excellent: it’s everything around it that leaks time.

📄

Scattered records

A child’s history lives in a binder, three Excel files, and someone’s memory. Pulling the full picture together takes an afternoon.

🌙

Evenings lost to admin

Session notes, reports, and invoices pile up for after hours. The workday ends long after the last child goes home.

🤔

“How is my child doing?”

Parents ask, and the honest answer is buried in paper. Trust slips when progress is hard to show.

In the therapy room

Capture the session in seconds: by voice or video

Instead of typing a report after hours, your therapist leaves a quick voice note or a short video clip right from their phone: attached to the session automatically.

🎤 Voice notes

Tap record and speak for a moment: an observation, a win to celebrate, a tip for home. Faster than typing, and the parent hears the warmth in your therapist’s voice.

🎬 Video clips

Record a short moment of a session: a new skill, a small breakthrough. Coordinators can review for quality; parents see real progress with their own eyes.

  • ✓ Private and secure by default

Meet Mira

Mira: your team’s AI clinical assistant

Built into TempoApp for your staff. Ask a question in plain language; Mira reads the real data and helps: so the thinking stays clinical and the busywork disappears.

🔍

Ask about any child

“How is Andrei progressing on requesting?” Mira pulls the real history and answers with concrete numbers and dates: no digging.

✍️

Drafts the writing

Session summaries, progress notes, and parent updates: drafted in seconds for your therapist to review, adjust, and send.

🧭

Suggests where to focus

Mira reads the progress and offers focus areas to consider: supporting your BCBA’s clinical judgment, never replacing it.

Private by design. Mira is for staff only: never parents. It only ever sees what it needs: children appear by initials, with no phone numbers or e-mail addresses.
