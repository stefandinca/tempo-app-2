**The notification system has two main parts:**
A. in-app notifications
B. push notifications

**A. In app notifications:**
1. appear in the dropdown of the notification bell
2. appear in the notification page (navigate to it by clicking show all in the notification bell dropdown)
3. show contextual content and redirect to the corresponding page.

**B. Push notifications:**
1. appear in the system notification depending on device
2. show contextual content and redirect to the corresponding page.


**Notification context for staff**
A staff member should receive notifications for:
1. Schedule changes: session created, updated or canceled
2. Attendance & Scores: Attendance logged and score updates
3. Team Updates: Team member assignments and changes
4. Client updates: New clients and profile changes
5. Billing Alers [Only for admin roles]: Invoice status change, payments and overdue notices 
6. New messages: when receiving a message from a staff member or a parent (A red dot with a number is also displayed on the Messages button in the left sidebar menu or the mobile menu)

A staff member can toggle receiving these notifications (applies both to push and in-app notifications) on and off in the settings page.

**Notification context for parents**
A parent should receive notifications for:
1. Schedule changes for his child: session created, updated or canceled.
2. Attendance & Scores: Attendance logged and score updates.
3. Billing Alerts: when an admin shares an invoice with them.
4. Document updates: when a staff member shares a document with them.
5. New messages: when receiving a message from a staff member (A red dot is also dispayed on the Messages button in the left sidebar menu or the mobile menu)

A parent can toggle receiving push notifications on and off in the settings page.
A parrent cannot toggle receiving in-app notifications.
